About

I am an undergraduate student at the University of Alberta studying asymptotic geometric analysis and high-dimensional probability. I will enter the PhD program in mathematics at the University of Washington in September 2026. My interests include centroids of convex bodies, log-concave measures, isotropy, and applications of geometric and probabilistic methods to theoretical computer science.
